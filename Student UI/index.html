Enrollment Procedure
  1. Register online.(LRN required)
  2. Fill out the vaccination form.(vaccinated applicants only)
  3. DOWNLOAD,PRINT & SIGN the Student's/Parent’s Guidelines Contract Form
    Downloadable Student Contract
  4. Submit your DOCUMENTS/ REQUIREMENTS to the Academy Registrar
  5. Settle your Fees
  6. ID Picture.(Wear WHITE COLLARED shirt ONLY. NO JEWELRIES. PROPER HAIR COLOR (BLACK). PROPER HAIRCUT IS REQUIRED)
  7. Proceed to the Academy Registrar's Office for Verification and Confirmation
  8. Check your Official Electronic Registration Form through your registered E-mail.
  9. Confirm your registration at the Academy Registrar
  10. Congratulations! You are now officially enrolled. WELCOME TO the NLAC family

Admission Requirements

  • A. JUNIOR/SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL (FOR NEW STUDENTS ONLY)
    • Photocopy of PSA Birth Certificate (4 copies with parent/guardian’s signature)
    • Good Moral Certificate
    • Original Copy of SF9/F138/ Report Card
    • Original Copy of SF10/F137 (claim request letter at the Academy Registrar’s Office)
    • ESC Certificate (for Grade 8-12 transferees from Private-ESC institutions only)
    • Recent 2x2 ID Picture (white background; 4 copies)
    • Photocopy of vaccination card (OPTIONAL; If the student is vaccinated)
    • Duly signed and accomplished Student’s/Parent’s Guidelines Contract Form. (Print your Contract form on a legal (8.5x13) sized bond paper for presentation and submission to the Academy Office.)
  • (For GRADE 7 Applicants ONLY) APPLICATION FOR ESC (Educational Service Contracting).

    • Latest Income Tax Return for the previous year or Certificate of Tax Exemption or Municipal Certification of Unemployment of Parents/Legal Guardian (for incoming Grade 7 only)
      NOTE: ESC Application Form will be provided by the Academy Registrar.

      For more details please visit our fb page to set an appointment with our working staff: NLAC Academy or visit the Academy Office.

  • B. OLD/RETURNING STUDENTS
    • Duly signed and accomplished Student’s/Parent’s Guidelines Contract Form. (Print your Contract form on a legal (8.5x13) sized bond paper for presentation and submission to the Academy Office)

  • C. FOR FOREIGN STUDENTS
    • Duly accomplished Student’s Personal History Statement(PHS)
    • A notarized affidavit of support or notarized notice of scholarship grant.
    • Authenticated student’s scholastic records by the Department of Foreign Affairs or counterpart agency from country of origin
    • Student passport and Birth Certificate
    • Letter of Sponsorship with contact number
